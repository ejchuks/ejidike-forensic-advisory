Bank Statement Forensics

Uncovering hidden anomalies, reconciliation fraud, and unauthorized ledger diversions before they paralyze your corporate cash flow.

The Vulnerability

Corrupt internal operators frequently exploit the structural gap between physical bank statements and internal cash books.

By manipulating reconciliation timelines, deliberately misclassifying expenses, or masking unapproved direct debits, millions of Naira can be seamlessly siphoned while standard automated software falsely reports a perfectly balanced ledger.

🛡️

Our Methodology

  • ✓
    Granular Extraction We bypass summary totals completely. We strip raw transaction data directly from gateway ledgers to prevent data tampering.
  • ✓
    Algorithmic Matching Reconciling thousands of daily cash inflows against dispatch logs and invoices to pinpoint the exact moment of fracture.
  • ✓
    Evidentiary Reporting Delivery of a court-ready, normalized ledger identifying the precise timestamp, method, and quantum of missing funds.
